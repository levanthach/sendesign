About Sen Design Studios

CONNECTING • SHARING • CREATING VALUE

Sen Design Studios was established in Vietnam.

Our company’s motto is based on the “Connecting – Sharing – Creating Value”, from there we are building up the human resource strategy and connecting our satellite companies based on the human factor.

Our major is hospitality and F&B. Besides, the style we want to focus on is Modern design that embraces the priceless value of culture and history in a contemporary perspective.

Our strengths are to combine Design with the other specialized fields such as Physics, Biology, Chemistry, Engineering, Market Research, Marketing Strategies, and etc., in order to creating a multiple perspectives for our project.

GIVING US THE OPPORTUNITY TO TURN YOUR DREAMS COME TRUE.
